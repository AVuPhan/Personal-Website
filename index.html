About Me

My name is Andy Phan and I am studying computer science at the University of St. Thomas. I am a 1st generation college student going into my senior year. I my heritage is from Vietname but I was born and raised in Richfield MN, a small suburb outside Minneapolis and later in my high school years I moved down to Burnville. I chose to attend St. Thomas because it was a Catholic university, it was close enough to home, and they gave me the best offer. After graduation, I hope to travel the world a bit before I start working. I hope to start a career at a company where the work I do is meaningful and impacts people's lives for the better.
