Friends & Brothers

College has been the most impactful experiences in my life. The most noteable thing was finding an amazing circle of people I trust and love to have fun with. All of my roommates are awesome. I love hanging out with them. Of the people in our house, I am the only one from Minnesota, and they are all from Green Bay. There is always that one sunday where the vikings play against the packers and that is the one day of the year we do not get along. Otherwise, we have made so many memories together and will make many more as the year goes on!

Outside of my friends, I have a whole group of brothers I also love hanging out with. I am a part of Sigma Chi, the only social fraternity here on campus. Those guys are ambitious students, possessed of good morals, are honorable, and are responsible, and are men of good character. And I am proud to be among them. This past spring we had our formal here on campus and it was a whole lot of fun. Over the summer we hung out together on the lake, trying to escape the chaos of our daily lives. Recently we started going to soccer games together and it has been a lot of fun cheering for the Minnesota Loons.



I love Food!

Merriam Webster defines 'foodie' as a 'person having an avid interest in a wide variety as well as the latest food fads.' I would consider myself to be a foodie because I love trying new food and I love to eat in general. Since living on my own, I've been experimenting with new recipies as well as trying all the restaurants around the twin cities. There are so many options and I am working my way through them all slowly but surely. As we all know, 'diet & excersise are a happy couple' so I try to hit the gym at least once a week to burn off all the calories I have stored up in my gut. My all time favorite food is Birria Tacos (pictured bottom center). The taco meat is slow cooked in a vegetable broth. Creamy cheese, garnishes, and shredded taco meat are placed on a crispy corn tortilla that has been fried in the broth. Then, when you eat it you dunk the taco in the broth for extra flavor. I always encourage everyone to try it because it will send your tastebuds to heaven.







Free Therapy

My parents grew up in a culture where pets aren't a normal thing. Sure, they had dogs in Vietnam but they were kept as yard pets. When they came to America they were suprised that people keep dogs in the house and also let them sleep in bed with them! I love dogs because they bring perPETual happiness so I am adopting a puppy as a graduation gift to myself. Pictured bottom right is my roommate's golden retriever. His name is Rudy and he is the goodest of good boys and I love it when his parents bring him to visit. Outside of dogs, I have a koi pond in my backyard at home. The man who sold our current house to my family was a viietnam vet & he built a japanese style garden in the backyard to kelp with his PTSD. He recovered very well so him and his wife moved somewhere warmer. Luckily he asked us if we wanted to keep the garden before he moved so we said yes. I have 12 koi and several koi fry in the pond. They love human attention and will swim toward you if they sense your presence. They are very majestic and I love to hangout by the pond with them.
