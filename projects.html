Eagle Scout Project (2018)

During my senior year of high school I was trying to earn my Eagle Scout Rank. One of its biggest challenges is the Eagle Scout Project. I had to plan, develop, and execute a community service project that makes a lasting difference. I started my search at my home parish to see what I could improve. What i noticed was the social hall & gradeschool gym area was about to be remoddled but there was a lot of prep work that needed to be done. At this point in time the parish couldn't remodel the area because they don't have the funds to hire movers to clear our 50 years worth of clutter and storage, and also parish volunteers were getting really old so they couldn't be much help. I decided that was going to be my project: to set up the social hall & gym for remodel thus saving my parish a lot of time and money. My scout troop and I reclocated the parish office, reorganized the garage, cleaned out the stage & storage area, and saved everything that needed to be saved in temporary storage. 2 days later my project was complete and I was one step closer to earning my Eagle Scout Rank.







M Health Fairview Internship (2022)

During the summer of 2022 I had the opportunity of interning at M Health Fairview as a network engineer. Over the course of my internship, I learned about fairview's network architecture & its fiber circuits. I leanred how to calibrate & monitor network switches, uninterupptible power supplies (UPS) and automatic transfer switches (ATS). I got to work in the data centers and upgrade the servers as well as all of the network closets in the 7 major hospitals. Most of all I got to eat some really good food.
